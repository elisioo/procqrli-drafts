How it works

From requisition to payment — fully automated.

1
Subscribe & Onboard

Your organization subscribes to a plan. Super Admin activates the tenant and your System Admin configures users, roles, and approval thresholds.

2
Accredit Vendors

Procurement Officers invite vendors through the external portal. Vendors submit documents, certifications, and capacity ratings for qualification.

3
Create & Approve Requisitions

Officers raise purchase requisitions. The ML engine flags vendor risk automatically. Managers approve through the multi-level workflow.

4
Track, Receive & Pay

POs are sent, deliveries tracked in real time, invoices matched, and payments disbursed — with full audit trails at every step.
